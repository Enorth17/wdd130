Pricing & FAQ

Pricing: Monday-Thursday: 1200 full day, 700 half day Friday-Sunday: 1800 full day Full Day pricing inculdes access to the building from 6am-12am and all amenities listed below Half Day pricing is only avaliale Monday-Thursday and can be rented for a 6 hour time block. Includes all amenities listed below Amenities included: Indoor and outdoor , bridal and groom suites, full kitchen, tables and chairs, sound system, slideshow tv, arch and backdrop pieces, accent tables and stands Add ons: Table cloths ($10 each), alcohol fee ($300), table set up and take down service ($100), decor and wedding planning service (starting at $800), cleaning services ($100) Frequenty Asked Questions: Do you offer hourly rates? We do! Reach out for pricing Does The Sage allow alcohol? Yes, but there is a $300 additional fee for alcohol Can we set up anything the night before? If the venue is not booked the night before your event we will allow a window of time for set up Can we use any vendor we'd like or do you have a specific approved list of vendors? You of course can use any vendor of your chooing, but we do have a list of vendors that we prefer and suggest What decorations come with booking? The decor that comes with your booking is tables and chairs, a slideshow tv, arch and backdrop pieces, and accent tables/stands. Table cloths are avalible to rent What is required to book? A %30 down payment and signed contract are required for booking Can I book The Sage for events other than a wedding? Of course! We welcome celebrations of all kind! Are garbage cans and bags provided? Yes! We want to give you everything needed to keep your party clean What is required for clean up? We ask that you leave the Sage looking as nice as you found it! By the end of your alotted time we ask that all tables and chairs are put away, the floor is swept, and all countertops are empty and wiped down
